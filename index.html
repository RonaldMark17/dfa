Processing Times

Fast and efficient passport processing with flexible options to suit your needs.

  • Regular: 10 working days (NCR)
  • Expedited: 5 working days (NCR)
  • Outside NCR: 12-7 working days
  • Special Lane: Priority access

Passport Validity

Know your passport validity period under Republic Act No. 10928 (2017).

  • Adults (18+): 10 years valid
  • Minors (under 18): 5 years valid

About DFA

Learn more about the Department of Foreign Affairs and our mission

Mission: To advance the national interest through the conduct of foreign policy and the promotion of economic, political, and cultural relations with other countries.

Vision: A world-class foreign service that serves the Filipino people with excellence and integrity.

Core Values: Integrity, Excellence, Patriotism, and Service to the Filipino people.

The Department of Foreign Affairs was established to manage the Philippines' international relations and represent Filipino interests abroad. With a rich history spanning decades, the DFA has evolved to meet the changing needs of the Filipino people in an increasingly interconnected world.

Philippine foreign policy is guided by the principles of national sovereignty, territorial integrity, and the promotion of peace and stability in the region. The DFA works to strengthen diplomatic ties and foster cooperation with nations worldwide.

The DFA is committed to providing high-quality services to all citizens. Our quality policy emphasizes efficiency, transparency, and customer satisfaction in all our operations and services.
